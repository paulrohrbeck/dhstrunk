Business Objects
  • Client’s Benefit
  • The term business objects signifies conceptual constructs in the enterprise repository which are assigned to recurring business tasks (like contract conclusion, financial status reporting). They encapsulate processing logic and database accesses from various applications. Their formalized definitions can be used to generate XML interfaces which make their business logic generally available. Thus, a homogeneous application layer is established which requires pure business expertise only without diverting the users’ attention to the underlying reality of heterogeneous technical systems.
  • Tasks Accomplished
  • design of a metastructure for the representation of business rules in the enterprise repoisitory
  • specification and test of a software tool for capturing, formalizing and syntax-checking of business rules
  • modelling business objects with a team of business experts.
